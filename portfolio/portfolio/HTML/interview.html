Interview

Here are an interview I made with my colleague (Toqa Abo shamah) and I have written a summary of the interview. Enjoy reading it!

What inspired you to choose software development as your field of study?

I have always been fascinated by technology and how software can solve real-world problems. The idea of building solutions that people use every day is what inspired me to pursue this field.

Which programming language do you enjoy the most, and why?

I enjoy Python the most because it is very versatile and has a simple syntax. It is great for quick prototyping and also powerful enough for complex projects like machine learning.

What do you think are the most important skills a software developer should have?

Problem-solving skills and the ability to think logically are crucial. Additionally, being adaptable and willing to learn new technologies is also very important in this ever-evolving field.

Can you describe a project you have worked on that you found particularly challenging?

One of the most challenging projects was developing a web app that integrated multiple APIs. Managing data from different sources while ensuring the apps performance was difficult but taught me a lot about back-end development and optimization.

How do you keep up with the latest trends and updates in software development?

I read tech blogs, follow industry leaders on social media, and take online courses. Staying connected with the developer community and attending workshops or lectures also helps me stay up-to-date.
