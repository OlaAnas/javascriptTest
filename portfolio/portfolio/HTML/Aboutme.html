My name is Ola Alkhousi,and since moving to the Netherlands in 2019, I've been dedicated to embracing new experiences, learning the Dutch language, and connecting with the communities around me.
Alongside my journey as a software development student, I have also engaged in several volunteer activities that have deeply enriched my personal and professional growth.

Community Engagement and Volunteering

Buddy to Buddy in Zutphen:

One of my first experiences was joining Buddy to Buddy, where I was paired with a wonderful Dutch lady. Together, we explored the city, practiced Dutch, and enjoyed photography,coffee, and attending local events.
This not only helped me improve my language skills but also introduced me to the beauty of Dutch culture through friendship.

Community Dinners in Maastricht:

To further my language skills and meet new people, I regularly attended community dinners in Maastricht. These gatherings were a fantastic way to engage with others in a relaxed environment while practicing conversational Dutch.

Serve the City Volunteer:

I also volunteered with Serve the City, helping organize activities and support people in need. This experience gave me a deeper understanding of community service and strengthened my commitment to making a positive impact wherever I can.

Arabic Language Volunteer Instructor:

During the pandemic, I volunteered to teach Arabic to students at Maastricht University who were interested in learning the language. This was a rewarding way to share my culture and language with others, despite the challenges of the global health crisis.

Education

Damascus University, Syria

Medical Studies | 1 year

I initially pursued medical studies at Damascus University, but due to the worsening situation in the city, it became impossible to continue living or studying there. This led me to immigrate to the Netherlands with my family for safety and a better future.

Dutch Language Courses

When I arrived in the Netherlands in 2019, my first goal was to learn Dutch. In 2020, I started my first B1-level Dutch course. However, due to the pandemic, I was unable to complete the course and had to relocate from Maastricht to Hardinxveld-Giessendam. Later, I successfully completed a B1 course online through Taalschool Rijswijk.

Erasmus University | Schakeljaar

I then attended a schakeljaar (transition year) at Erasmus University, where I earned certifications in Wiskunde B (Math), English (IELTS), and the Staatsexamen B2 Dutch.

Hogeschool Rotterdam | Informatica

I briefly studied Informatica at Hogeschool Rotterdam, where I learned Python through a basecamp and worked with micro:bit devices. However, due to personal circumstances, I had to discontinue this program.

Techniek College Rotterdam | Software Development (MBO 4)

Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Software Development at Techniek College Rotterdam (MBO 4 level), where I am focusing on developing my skills in programming and technology.

Online Courses and Self-Learning

HTML and CSS | Khan Academy

While living in the AZC in Maastricht,
I took an online course in HTML and CSS on Khan Academy. This was my first introduction to web development, and it laid the foundation for my future in technology.

Full Stack Development | Akoan

During the pandemic, alongside my Dutch language courses and schakeljaar, I completed a full stack development course through an organization called Akoan. This course opened my eyes to the complexity and excitement of computer science, leading me to switch my career focus from medicine to technology.

Cybersecurity Lecture | Exact

I attended a lecture on cybersecurity at Exact, where I learned about their training methods and how they approach security in their systems. It was an enriching experience that deepened my interest in the field.

CS50's Introduction to Computer Science | EdX

Currently, alongside my studies at Techniek College Rotterdam, I am self-studying CS50's Introduction to Computer Science through the EdX platform. This course has given me a solid understanding of the fundamentals of computer science.
